Maturity of Global Goods: Summary Table

This table reflects the maturity model baseline assessments and the historical reassessment of global goods funded through Digital Square. All maturity model assessments are *self-reported* by the funded organization leading the software development of the global good. For more information about global good maturity models, please contact Carl Fourie at cfourie@path.org.
