Digital Square Maturity Model Details

To help identify areas of investments for global goods, Digital Square has developed maturity models for 2 of the types of global goods:

  • Software Global Good Maturity Model for Software digital health tools.
  • Content Global Good Maturity Model for Content Global Goods.

We continue to engage the digital health community, in particular the Digital Health and Interoperability Working Group of the Health Data Collaborative, in the development of this model.

Maturity Model: Software Global Good

Currently, the Software Global Good maturity model is in version 1.4, last updated August 2022. The initial version (1.1) was presented at the Global Digital Health Forum in December of 2017.

Maturity Model: Content Global Good

Currently, the Content Global Good maturity model is in version 1.0, last updated June 2023.
